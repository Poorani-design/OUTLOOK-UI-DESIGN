To:Me,james,ali@gmail.com

25 November 2013| 10:24 PM

Hi Eric.,

There are things I love and hate about Aussie food.

Being American, I loathe Australian hamburgers and lack of quality Mexican food. I love the amount of Asian restaurants available in the cities, and you take me out for a pub dinner (schnitzel) any day of the week. My fascination with Australia started yound and was probably enhanced as tween when I fell in love with Silverchair. I always thought it sounded like an exotic place to travel, and you never meet an Aussie travelling that doesn't love their home country.


Thank you..
